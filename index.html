Introducing Simon

Founder & CEO

A Lawyer with a lifetime's experience in construction, it was congruous to focus on construction law after becoming a Lawyer.

With 30 years experience working in Central Australia constructing roads, buildings and public infrastructure, and eventually specialising on 'difficult' construction projects, I have a breadth of knowledge of the difficulties and injustices faced daily by construction and contract workers.
I also have intimate knowledge and understanding of the building and construction environment, terminologies, and the unfair practices often employed against hard-working Contractors. Now, as a Lawyer, I also know the law, how to best apply it to individual circumstances, and receive great satisfaction helping clients with my in-depth understanding of their problem. Contractors that are seeking a legal solution to their problem need both legal and industry-knowledgeable help. I have that rare combination of assets, which enables me to truly understand the issues of a case so that I can work with clients to develop a suitable solution.
