Building and construction industry

Key legislation & initiatives

The South Australian Government provides online access to the various Building and Construction Legislation. Some of the key legislation that may be of interest to businesses in the construction industry includes:

  • Planning, Development and Infrastructure Act 2016
  • Competition and Consumer Act 2010
  • Development Regulations 2008
  • Building Work Contractors Act 1995

    • The above list is minimal and other regulations and legislation govern the building and construction industry. The following list has a few examples of broader, but relevant, codes and legislation, highlighting the expansive governance umbrella shading the industry.

    Australian Consumer Law (ACL)
    National Construction Code (includes Building and Plumbing codes)
    Disability (Access to Premises-Buildings) Standards 2010
    Environment Protection and Biodiversity Conservation Act 1999
    Heritage protection
    Native title legislation
    State Building Acts.
